We Bike

Movie is as a result of a group work. Based on current materials of “I bike Copenhagen” we created a short campaign video that promotes cycling around Copenhagen and gives some tips on how to bike safely. The movie is a specially dedicated to newcomers, who feel a bit lost and are afraid of cycling in the city. Our idea was to make them less worried and encourage them to join the community of cyclist.

Interaction

In the movie we present interviews with people living in Copenhagen who use bikes as their main form of transportation. To bring the message across the target audience we used ethos rhetoric, which turned out to be a good way of communication in this case. The movie seems to be credible and convincing. Used rhetorical arguments should motivate newcomers to bike in the city.

Following created by the group storyboard I participated in taking shots. I was focus on filming bikes and the city environment, while the rest of the group was working on interviews. I also took part in the post-production, mostly by helping with selecting of the suitable shots and arranging them together.

The website is created by using HTML5 and CSS. It is responsive, which in this case means that it is compatible with large, medium and small devices. While designing the website I was using the CSS Grid Layout Module, that turned out to be a very practical and useful tool. The Grid Layout allowed me to change positions of the content easily, so that it could be quickly adjust to different devices. Although Grid Layout worked fine for me in that case, I still had some issues with sizes of items displayed in the website. I would like to improve that if future.
